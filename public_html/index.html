About Depression

Symptoms of depression may last for 2 weeks or more, occurring nearly every day, and may include:

  • (feeling sad, empty, hopeless, or on the Loss of interest or pleasure in activities you once enjoyedverge of tears)
  • Weight loss or experiencing weight gain, decreases or increases appetite
  • Difficulty sleeping or being overly tired
  • Restlessness or feeling slowed down
  • A lack of concentration or difficulty with decision making
  • Thoughts of death or suicide
  • It is important to know that antidepressants may increase suicidal thoughts

It is important to know that Antidepressant medicines may increase suicidal thoughts or actions in some children, teenagers, or young adults within the first few months of treatment.Patients on antidepressants and their families or caregivers should watch for new or worsening depression symptoms, especially sudden changes in mood, behaviors, thoughts, or feelings. This is very important when an antidepressant medicine is started or when the dose is changed. Report any changes in these symptoms immediately to the doctor.
